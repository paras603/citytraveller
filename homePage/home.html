Season Highlights

The Annapurna, one of Nepal's most well-known mountain ranges, is made up of numerous peaks of differing elevations. At about 8000 meters, Annapurna I and II are the tallest of them, offering visitors to the Himalayas a breathtaking panoramic perspective.

Annappurna Mountain

One of the peaks in the Himalayan mountain range is Mount Everest. It is situated between Tibet, an autonomous territory of China, and Nepal. At 29,032 feet, or 8,849 meters, it is regarded as the highest point on Earth. George Everest, a former Surveyor General of India, was honored with a name change for the peak in the nineteenth century.

Mt. Everest

Nepal's capital city is Kathmandu. Here, you may stroll through ancient Buddhist and Hindu temples and monasteries and learn about thousands of years of cultural traditions. The city is located in central Nepal's bowl-shaped Kathmandu Valley at a height of around 1,400 meters (4,600 feet).
